Design Kosova (DKF) operates as an umbrella platform for six core initiatives. Each activates a different layer of Kosovo's creative ecosystem â from annual festivals to educational programmes, marketplaces, and urban interventions.

We believe design is a public resource. Not reserved for institutions or corporations, but a tool that belongs to communities, cities, and individuals. Our work puts design at the service of Kosovo's cultural transformation.

Rooted in Pristina but globally connected â DKF has built formal partnerships with Design Academy Eindhoven, Porto Design Factory, the World Design Organization, and iF Design Foundation.

One Week That Changes Everything

The Festival of Design is Kosovo's most significant cultural design event. Each year it transforms Pristina into a stage for design thinking, making, and dialogue â drawing practitioners from across the region and world.

The festival operates across multiple venues, from galleries and public squares to industrial spaces. Every edition is built around a central theme that responds to Kosovo's evolving cultural moment.

What It Includes
  • Public exhibitions across Pristina's cultural venues
  • International speaker programme and keynote talks

Where Ideas Become Objects

The Factory of Design is DKF's research and making arm. It commissions applied design projects that respond to Kosovo's most pressing cultural, social, and urban challenges.

The Factory brings together designers, architects, engineers, and cultural thinkers in cross-disciplinary teams. Projects range from product prototypes and spatial interventions to design-led policy proposals.

What It Includes
  • Commissioned design research projects
  • Collaborative studio residencies
  • Prototyping support and fabrication access
  • Cross-disciplinary teams and methodology
  • Exhibition of outcomes at Festival of Design

Design for the City

The Urban Design Lab applies design thinking to Pristina's most visible challenge: its public realm. Working with municipalities, communities, and urban planners, we co-design public spaces that reflect and serve Kosovo's people.

Our approach is participatory â we believe the people who use a space should have a voice in how it's designed. Every project begins with community consultation and ends with public accountability.

What It Includes
  • Public space redesign with city and community partners
  • Community co-design workshops and consultation
  • Research into Kosovo's built environment and heritage
  • Collaboration with Ministry of Urban Planning
  • Exhibition of proposals at Festival of Design
